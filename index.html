Engage with your target audience on auto-pilot.

Alecko is an app that engages with your target instagram audience automatically. Because she is digital, she can do this all day, while you focus on your brand's content.

Detailed targetting.

Find your brand's target audience by hashtags, instagram places, followers of an account, likers or commenters of a post etc... Zone in to the exact type of people that would enjoy your content.

Smart engagement.

Bring the attention of your target audience to your brand's instagram account by engaging with them through Alecko's smart automatic actions such as liking their posts and or following them.

Account safety.

Alecko engages with your target audience just like a normal human would, (hence the human name). Your account is therefore kept safe from instagram action blocks.

Intuitive.

Define your target audience and how to engage with them through the app's simple and intuitive interface that is easy to understand and work with.

How The App Works?

Sign-Up & Connect account

Sign up to the app then connect your instagram account. Your instagram credentials stay with you, they are not saved in the cloud.

Create an audience

Define where to find your target audience by creating an audience, here you can get really specific by targetting hashtags, places, followers of an account, likers of a post etc... You can create multiple audiences.

Create & Activate a campaign

Define how to engage with the audience you have created by creating a campaign, here you can choose whether to like their posts, follow them or even unfollow them, once you activate your campaign, Alecko starts engaging with your people.

Step back & focus on your business

Once your campaign is active, you can step back and let Alecko engage with your people, while you focus on producing good content for your page, that will turn the people who come back to your account, into customers/followers of your brand.

Driving attention to your brand's account

Alecko helps you introduce your brand to your target audience by engaging with them automatically.

Target by hashtags

You can find your target audience in as many hashtags as you can need, Alecko will then engage with the people who post under those hashtags.

Target by places

If your target audience relates with a particular place(s), you can target them by these places, Alecko will engage with the people who post under that place.

Target by accounts

You can target the audience of similar brands to yours, Alecko can engage with their followers, likers, commenters or even the people they follow.

Target by posts

Your target audience may be engaging with a certain post, Alecko can engage with the people that have liked or commented on that post.

Multiple audiences

You can create as many audiences as you like in the app, Each one could be targetting different groups of people for different purposes.

Multiple campaigns

You can create and activate multiple campaigns within the app, Each one could be engaging differently with different audiences for different reasons.
